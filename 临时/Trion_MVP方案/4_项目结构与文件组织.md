```xml

  <PropertyGroup>
    <TargetFramework>net472</TargetFramework>
    <AssemblyName>TrionMVP</AssemblyName>
    <RootNamespace>TrionMVP</RootNamespace>
    <LangVersion>latest</LangVersion>

    <Reference Include="Assembly-CSharp">

      <Private>false</Private>
    </Reference>
    <Reference Include="Verse">
      <HintPath>C:\NiwtGames\Steam\steamapps\common\RimWorld\RimWorldWin64_Data\Managed\Verse.dll</HintPath>
      <Private>false</Private>
    </Reference>
    <Reference Include="UnityEngine.CoreModule">
      <HintPath>C:\NiwtGames\Steam\steamapps\common\RimWorld\RimWorldWin64_Data\Managed\UnityEngine.CoreModule.dll</HintPath>
      <Private>false</Private>
    </Reference>
  </ItemGroup>
```

## 常见问题排查

### 编译报错："找不到ProjectTrion"
**解决**：确保.csproj的HintPath指向正确的ProjectTrion.dll位置

### 游戏无法加载Mod
**排查**：
1. 检查LoadFolders.xml
2. 检查About.xml gameVersion
3. 查看RimWorld日志 `~\AppData\LocalLow\Ludeon Studios\RimWorld by Ludeon Studios\Player.log`

### Hediff无法植入
**排查**：
1. 确认HediffDef defName与代码引用一致
2. 确认Surgery能成功执行
3. 检查Hediff_TrionGland.PostAdd是否被调用

### 天赋无法初始化
**排查**：
1. 检查TrionMVP_ModComponent.TalentCapacityProvider是否设置
2. 检查Building_TrionDetector.ScanPawn逻辑
3. 查看日志"首次扫描"消息
